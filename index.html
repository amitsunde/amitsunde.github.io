  Amit Satish Unde

I am currently pursuing my postdoctoral research under the supervision of Dr. Renu M. Rameshan in the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering, IIT Mandi. My current research topics include multi-object tracking and segmentation (MOTS), trajectory forecasting, and light field imaging. I have completed my Ph.D. in the area of compressive sensing and multimedia security under the supervision of Dr. Deepthi P. P. in the Dept. of Electronics and Communication Engineering, NIT, Calicut, Kerala, India. I received the B.E. Degree in Electronics Engineering from JJMCOE Jaysingpur (Shivaji University, Kolhapur) and M.Tech. in Signal Processing from NIT Calicut.
